

1. Define Your MVP (Must Work)

Many teams lose because they build too much.

Your MVP should be:

Member Management

Create member

Store BVN/phone

Create 2 virtual accounts automatically

Savings VA

Loan VA



Savings Tracking

Receive money through webhook

Update balance

Create ledger entry


Loan Management

Create loan

Disburse loan

Repay loan

Calculate balance


Reports

Total savings

Total loans

Defaulters

Export CSV


If these 4 modules work perfectly, you already have a demo-worthy product.

2. Design the Database Properly

Add these collections.

penalties

{
  loanId,
  memberId,
  amount,
  reason,
  createdAt
}


---

repayments

{
  loanId,
  amountPaid,
  principalPaid,
  interestPaid,
  penaltyPaid,
  paymentRef,
  createdAt
}

This makes reporting easier.


---

auditlogs

{
  actor,
  action,
  entity,
  entityId,
  timestamp
}

Judges love audit trails.

Example:

Admin created loan
Admin disbursed loan
Member repayment received

7. Handle Every Payment Scenario

The biggest differentiator of your project.

Exact Payment

Due: 55,000
Paid: 55,000

Status: PAID


---

Underpayment

Due: 55,000
Paid: 30,000

Remaining: 25,000
Penalty: 1,250
Status: ARREARS


---

Overpayment

Due: 55,000
Paid: 100,000

45,000 reduces principal


---

Duplicate Webhook

Receive webhook twice

Process once
Ignore second


---

Wrong Account

Savings payment sent to Loan VA

Create policy:

Auto-apply to loan
or
Flag for review
